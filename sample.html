A Shamanic forest where incense fumes infuse the purest sap.

Darkness makes the cedars with their thousand-year-old roots loom even larger. Wrapped in nocturnal coolness, the cedars are sentries of time, exuding their dry, resinous scent.

CREPUSCULUM MIRABILE

The burning heat of jasmine at twilight.

Come the dusk, it’s hard to believe that jasmine’s tiny petals harbour such a powerful nighttime fragrance. The jasmine awaits its mysterious evening visitors: fluttering hummingbirds who get drunk on its pollen.

Fragrance notes:

  • Jasmine

Arcana Rosa

This rose likes to change attire at nightfall — sometimes feminine, sometimes masculine.

Drawn out of its lethargy by the dusk, this strange rose, bristling with thorns, is set ablaze on a fire of vetiver roots and gaïac wood. It exudes a smoky murmur that bewitches anyone who approaches, thus keeping its secret.

Fragrance notes:

  • Clove

  • Bulgarian Rose

  • Guaiac Wood

Venenum

The sensation of danger is as sweet as it is enveloping.

The vines that snake around the trunk of the sandalwood tree appear to be perfectly poisonous — but this is only theatre! Their juice is delicate, and their soft, milky aroma is savoured by forest wildlife in the summer evenings of eternal India.

Fragrance notes:

  • Masala Chai Tea

  • Rice

  • Sandalwood
